Lets dive into Graphs: What are they?

What are graphs?

Graphs are data structures that represent relationships between objects, made up of nodes and edges.

Directed Graph

Edges have a direction, similar to arrows. Like the Instagram followers.

Undirected Graph

Edges have no direction. Example: Facebook friendships.

Weighted Graph

Edges have an associated weight or value. Liike the Google Maps distances.
